DIY AWS Cost Cutting

There are two parts to this, both crucial. You need to become aware of what is going on in your account – whether by clicking around the AWS Console, using the AWS CLI, or running a report generator like the one we made. You need to know things like:

  • What services are my costs coming from?
  • What usage types are my costs coming from?
  • What instances do I have running? Am I actually utilizing the resources?

Our Cloud Cost Mapping Script does all of this for you. You can also use AWS Cost Explorer, the EC2 console and the CloudWatch console, but it will take longer.

Once you know where your costs are coming from, run through our playbooks for each AWS service. Start with the service you’re spending the most on, then move down to maximize savings.

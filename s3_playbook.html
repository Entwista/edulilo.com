S3 Cost Cutting Playbook

  1. Enable S3 Intelligent Tiering

    This lets you keep the same data but pay less over time as objects age and are accessed less. It's really simple to set up and the savings can be a massive proportion of your bill. If you run our reporting script, it will show you whether you have a lot of objects that should probably be moved to a cheaper storage tier.

  2. Use CloudFront for heavy data‑out traffic

    If you're spending a lot on data transfer out through a web application, front your content with Amazon CloudFront. Caching at the edge can drastically reduce S3 egress costs and improve performance.

  3. Retain most data until Glacier costs dominate

    You can think about deleting data, but until Glacier and Deep Archive make up a significant part of your storage cost, it's usually better to keep most of it around. This avoids data-loss risk while still capturing the bulk of cost savings.
